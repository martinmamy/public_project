FancyLearn recognizes and rewards users who consistently demonstrate expertise, accuracy, and meaningful contributions through its Expert Verification system. This status is granted to individuals who have built trust within the community by providing reliable, high-quality knowledge and actively helping others.

1. Purpose of Expert Verification

The Expert Verification program is designed to:

  • Identify and highlight knowledgeable contributors
  • Increase trust and credibility within the community
  • Promote high-quality answers and engagement
  • Provide recognition for consistent performance
2. Eligibility Criteria

To qualify for Expert Verification, users must meet all of the following requirements:

  • Reputation Score: A minimum of 500 points is required. This reflects overall contribution quality, engagement, and trust within the platform.
  • Accepted Answers: At least 5 answers must be marked as accepted. Accepted answers indicate that your solutions have directly helped resolve user problems.
  • Top-Voted Contributions: A minimum of 10 highly-rated answers is required. These are determined based on community voting and engagement signals such as upvotes.

All criteria must be satisfied simultaneously. Meeting only part of the requirements does not qualify for verification.

3. Evaluation Process

Eligibility is evaluated automatically based on platform data, including reputation, accepted answers, and voting activity.

Once all criteria are met, verification may be granted automatically or reviewed before approval to ensure compliance with platform standards.

4. Verification Fee

In certain cases, a one-time verification fee may be required to finalize the process.

$15 USD — one-time Expert Verification fee

Payments are securely processed through third-party providers. FancyLearn does not store or access sensitive financial information.

5. Benefits of Verification
  • Increased profile visibility across the platform
  • Enhanced credibility and trust from other users
  • Priority access to opportunities such as consultations or collaborations
  • Potential eligibility for monetization features
6. Maintaining Verified Status

Verified status is not permanent and depends on continued compliance with platform rules.

FancyLearn reserves the right to revoke verification if a user:

  • Violates community guidelines or platform policies
  • Engages in misleading, harmful, or low-quality contributions
  • Attempts to manipulate reputation, votes, or engagement systems
  • Abuses platform features or monetization systems
7. Transparency and Fairness

All verification decisions are based strictly on measurable activity and platform data. We aim to ensure that the process remains fair, objective, and consistent for all users.

8. Updates to Criteria

FancyLearn may update verification criteria, thresholds, or processes as the platform evolves. Any changes will be reflected in this policy.

FancyLearn provides an advertising system that allows users to promote content, services, or external platforms directly within the ecosystem. Ads are designed to remain relevant, non-intrusive, and aligned with the educational nature of the platform.

1. Creating Advertisements

Users can create advertisements through the Ads Dashboard by submitting the following:

  • An optional image or visual banner
  • A selected duration (in days)

Advertisements are initially created in a pending state and require payment before becoming active.

💡 Note: You must link a valid PayPal account before creating ads.
2. Pricing Model

Advertising costs are calculated based on duration:

  • Fixed rate per day (e.g., $2/day)
  • Total cost = duration × daily rate

Payment is processed securely through PayPal. Once payment is confirmed, the advertisement becomes active immediately.

3. Ad Placement & Targeting

FancyLearn allows ads to be displayed in contextually relevant locations across the platform:

  • Problem-Based Targeting: Ads can be associated with specific problems or categories, ensuring they appear to users interested in that topic.
  • Fallback Ads: If no targeted ads are available, general ads may be displayed.
  • Post Integration: Ads may appear alongside problems, answers, or feeds in a non-disruptive format.
💡 Tip: Target ads to specific problem categories to increase relevance and improve engagement.
4. Performance Tracking

Advertisers can track the performance of their ads through the dashboard.

Impressions are automatically counted when ads are displayed, and clicks are tracked when users interact with them.

5. Monetization Opportunities

Advertisements can be used strategically to:

The Promotion System governs how content is distributed and ranked across the platform, including feeds, search results, trending sections, and recommendation surfaces. It combines algorithmic ranking, engagement signals, and optional user-paid visibility boosts.

Promotion features are subject to platform rules, eligibility checks, and system constraints. Certain actions may require account verification, sufficient wallet balance, and compliance with content quality standards before activation.
Overview of Promotion Status
  • Active Promotion: When a post is promoted, it receives temporary enhanced visibility in feeds and discovery sections. Each promotion has a defined duration and automatically expires once its lifecycle ends.
  • Bounty Influence: Posts with active bounties may receive additional visibility due to increased user engagement and participation incentives. Once the bounty ends, ranking returns to normal.
Promotion Tiers & Visibility Levels
  • 📌 Pinned Highest Visibility Tier

    Pinned content is permanently prioritized at the top of feeds and bypasses normal ranking logic. It is reserved for high-impact, system-critical, or manually approved content.

    May be assigned by administrators or special system rules. Cannot be overridden by other promotions.
  • 🚀 Boosted High Visibility Boost

    Boosted content receives increased distribution across feeds and recommendation sections. It competes with other boosted content based on engagement performance.

    Time-limited and automatically expires when duration ends.
  • ⭐ Featured Algorithmic Highlight

    Featured content is selected based on quality signals such as engagement rate, relevance, and user interaction metrics. It improves discoverability in trending sections.

    Rotates dynamically based on algorithmic evaluation.
Eligibility Requirements
  • Only the original author can initiate promotions
  • An active and verified payment method may be required
  • Sufficient wallet balance must be available for selected promotion tier
  • Each promotion type can only be active once per content item at a time
  • Promotions cannot exceed defined system limits or stacking rules
Ranking Behavior
  • Pinned: Overrides all ranking and appears first in feeds
  • Boosted: High ranking weight with temporary visibility surge
  • Featured: Moderate algorithmic boost based on quality signals
  • Bounty Content: Temporary increase in visibility during bounty period
System Processing Logic
  • Promotion activation requires validation of eligibility rules
  • Each promotion is assigned a time-based expiration timestamp
  • Feed ranking engine recalculates visibility dynamically
  • Engagement metrics continuously influence ranking strength
Fair Use & Abuse Prevention
  • Automated detection prevents spam or artificial boosting behavior
  • Fake engagement signals are filtered and penalized
  • Expired promotions are automatically removed from ranking calculations
  • Stacking or manipulation of promotion levels is strictly prevented
Transparency Notice
Promotion improves visibility but does not guarantee engagement or interaction. Final content ranking is determined by a combination of algorithmic scoring, user behavior, and content quality evaluation.

The FancyLearn feed is designed to surface the most relevant, high-quality, and engaging content based on user activity, contributions, and interactions. The system uses multiple ranking strategies to ensure that valuable knowledge is prioritized and easily discoverable.

1. Types of Feeds
  • Global Feed: Displays problems created by the user, ordered by recency. This helps users track their own activity, contributions, and performance over time.
  • Trending Feed: Highlights top-performing problems based on a computed score combining:
    • Votes (community approval)
    • Number of answers (engagement)
    • Views (visibility and reach)
    This feed prioritizes content gaining traction across the platform.
  • Followers Feed: Shows users who follow you, allowing visibility into your audience and network growth.
  • Following Feed: Displays users you follow, helping you stay updated with their activity and contributions.
  • Personalized Feed: Displays content from users you follow only if you have interacted with them (e.g., answered their questions or voted on their content). This ensures highly relevant and meaningful content tailored to your behavior.
2. Ranking & Visibility Factors

Feed visibility is not random. Content is ranked dynamically using measurable signals:

  • Engagement: Votes, answers, comments, and clicks increase visibility.
  • Views: Frequently viewed problems gain higher exposure in trending feeds.
  • Answer Activity: Problems with multiple high-quality answers rank higher.
  • User Reputation: Contributions from high-reputation users are more likely to surface.
  • Consistency: Regular contributors are favored over inactive users.
3. Content Quality Guidelines
  • Problems should be clear, specific, and well-structured
  • Answers must provide real value (not generic or copied content)
  • Titles and descriptions should reflect actual content
  • No spam, repetition, or artificially inflated engagement
  • Content must not be misleading, harmful, or irrelevant
4. Monetization Impact on Feed

Certain monetization features can influence visibility and engagement:

  • Bounties: Problems with bounties attract more answers and engagement, increasing feed visibility.
  • Tips: High-quality answers that receive tips signal value and may gain more exposure.
  • Ad Integration: Ads may appear alongside feed content but do not override organic ranking quality.

Financial activity (tips and bounties) is tracked and may be reflected in analytics dashboards, helping users optimize their contributions for both impact and earnings.

5. Personalization Logic

The feed adapts to your behavior using interaction-based filtering:

  • Content from users you follow is prioritized
  • Only interacted content (votes, answers) appears in personalized feeds
  • Inactive or irrelevant connections are automatically deprioritized
6. Analytics & Insights

FancyLearn provides analytics to help you understand your feed performance:

  • Tips Analytics: Track earnings and spending over time from tips.
  • Bounties Analytics: Monitor rewards earned and bounties created.
  • Financial Summary: View net earnings (tips + bounties) to measure contribution value.
7. Violations & Enforcement
  • Low-quality or spam content may be removed
  • Artificial engagement (fake votes, spam answers) may lead to penalties
  • Feed visibility can be reduced for repeated violations
  • Accounts may be restricted or suspended in severe cases
8. Best Practices to Grow in the Feed
  • Post clear, high-value problems that invite meaningful answers
  • Provide detailed, well-structured answers
  • Engage with other users (vote, comment, follow)
  • Use bounties strategically to boost engagement
  • Stay consistent—activity over time improves visibility
