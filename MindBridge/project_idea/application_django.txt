FancyLearn — Complete Application Prototype

FancyLearn is a modern educational Q&A, problem-solving, and creator-monetization platform inspired by Stack Overflow, Reddit, Medium, and Patreon, designed specifically for students, teachers, professionals, and experts.

1. Product Vision
Core Goal

Allow users to:

Ask academic or professional questions
Answer and teach others
Build reputation
Earn money from expertise
Follow experts
Access premium educational content
Join communities around topics
